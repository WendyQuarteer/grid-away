Maya Bay, Thailand

Cliffs tower more than 100 metres above this bay, lush greenery provides the backdrop and soft white sand leads down to crystal clear water – pure paradise.

Thailand
Dalawella Beach, Sri Lanka

Palm trees bend over the golden sand that leads down to the wavy turquoise water. This stretch of sand is primarily known for its insta-famous rope swing.

Sri Lanka
Dhigurah Island, Maldives

you’re unlikely to find a beach that you wouldn’t describe as perfect here. You’ll instantly conjure up images of the water bungalows that sit atop the turquoise sea that laps the white sand.

Maldives
Honopu Beach, Hawaii

Also goes by the nickname ‘Cathedral Beach.’ The stretch of golden sand is situated within the Na Pali Coast State Wilderness Park and is the most secluded and remote beach in Hawaii.

Hawaii
Seven Mile Beach, Jamaica

Look down the coastline here and white sand is lapped by clear water that glistens in the sunlight as far as the eye can see.

Jamaica
Sandy Lane Beach, Barbados

This beach has been described as the ‘playground of the rich and famous’ – celebrities and royalty have been soaking up the sun on its pure white sand for many years.

Barbados
Ile aux Cerfs Beach, Mauritius

you’ll struggle to find a beach that you wouldn’t describe as idyllic. However, there’s something romantic about this beach, which is situated on an island within the country’s biggest lagoon and can only be reached by boat.

Mauritius
Manuel Antonio, Costa Rica

It’s part of Manuel Antonio National Park, near Quepos in North Puntarenas on the Pacific Coast.

Costa Rica
